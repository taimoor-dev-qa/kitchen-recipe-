Fresh Garden Salad

A light, crunchy, and refreshing salad packed with fresh vegetables — a perfect healthy side or light meal on its own.

Cooking Time

10 Minutes

Servings

2 People

Calories

150 kcal

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red Onion
  • Olives
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumber.
  2. Slice the red onion thinly and add to the bowl.
  3. Toss all vegetables together with olives and feta cheese.
  4. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad.
  5. Mix gently and serve fresh.
