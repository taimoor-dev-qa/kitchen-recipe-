Strawberry Custard

A creamy, chilled dessert made with smooth custard and fresh strawberries — light, sweet, and perfect for any occasion.

Cooking Time

25 Minutes

Servings

4 People

Calories

220 kcal

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Custard Powder
  • Strawberries
  • Vanilla Essence

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk in a pan over medium heat until warm.
  2. Mix custard powder with a little cold milk to make a smooth paste.
  3. Add the paste into the warm milk along with sugar, stirring continuously.
  4. Cook until the custard thickens, then add vanilla essence and let it cool.
  5. Chop the strawberries, layer them with the custard, and chill before serving.
