Smoky BBQ Chicken

Juicy grilled chicken glazed with a rich, smoky homemade BBQ sauce — a crowd favorite for weekend cookouts and family dinners.

Cooking Time

40 Minutes

Servings

4 People

Calories

380 kcal

Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paprika
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and lemon juice.
  2. Let it rest in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill and brush it lightly with olive oil.
  4. Grill chicken, basting with BBQ sauce every few minutes.
  5. Cook until fully done and slightly charred, then serve hot.
