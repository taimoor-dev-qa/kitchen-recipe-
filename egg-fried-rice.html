Egg Fried Rice

A quick and flavorful fried rice loaded with fluffy eggs, fresh vegetables, and a savory soy sauce touch — perfect for a fast weeknight meal.

Cooking Time

20 Minutes

Servings

3 People

Calories

310 kcal

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Green Peas
  • Spring Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Cooking Oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add carrots and green peas, cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Push vegetables aside, scramble the eggs in the pan.
  4. Add cooked rice and soy sauce, mix everything well.
  5. Garnish with spring onion and serve hot.
